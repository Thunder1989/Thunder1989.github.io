README

This dataset contains the metadata (or point name) and timeseries data (one week's worth) of streams from three office buildings: one from the University of Virginia and two from UC Berkeley.

We manually created the type ground truth (e.g., temperature, humidity, etc) for all the streams.

If you have further questions, feel free to contact me at hong AT virginia DOT edu.

The dataset is available here.
